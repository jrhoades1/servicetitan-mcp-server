# Picking Up 2 More Jobs Per Week — $57K in Free Revenue

## The Opportunity

We're averaging **93 jobs/week** across the team. If we pick up just **2 more per week**, that's **$57,200/year in additional revenue** with zero added cost — no new trucks, no new techs.

| | Current | With +2/Wk |
|--|--:|--:|
| Annual revenue | $2,748,522 | $2,805,722 |
| **Added revenue** | | **+$57,200** |
| Net after tech commission | | +$43,472 |

---

## Where the 2 Jobs Are Hiding

Neill averages **2.7 jobs/day** (~24 hrs/wk including drive time) — the team averages **3.7**. He's going home early because the quarterly bonus cushions him. That bonus lets him skip ~2 jobs/week and still clear $95K.

> *Hours include ~1 hr/day portal-to-portal drive time. ServiceTitan on-site time shows ~19 hrs/wk, but techs are paid home-to-home.*

---

## One Simple Fix

**Tie the quarterly bonus to a daily job minimum.**

- Must average **3.5 jobs/day** to get the full 3% quarterly bonus
- Below 3.0 jobs/day = no bonus

That's it. No commission changes. No new policies. Most techs already qualify — it only changes behavior for the ones leaving early.
