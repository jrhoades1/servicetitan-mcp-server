## 4. What I'm Proposing

**Bump my base to $95K and add a small bonus tied to team revenue.**

- Base goes from $89K to **$95K** — when prices went up 6.3%, tech pay went up automatically through commission. Mine didn't. This just catches me up the same way
- Replace my current **$2,500/quarter flat bonus** with **0.5% of quarterly revenue** — that's about **$1,600 more per quarter** at current revenue, and it only increases if the business grows
- Total comp lands around **$111K** — still under what the market pays for this role

This isn't a big swing. A straight catch-up raise for the 6% pricing increase alone would be $94,340. I'm just asking for a little above that, with some of it tied to how the business does.

- And it means we don't have to have this conversation again every year — it adjusts naturally
